Introduction of DAIICHI KASEI

Precision Plastic Part Business Group

Daiichi Kasei Co., Ltd. (IKKA), a subsidiary invested by JABON INTERNATIONAL, is a Japanese manufacturer of components for auto parts and mold modules. Its overseas bases of production and sales include China (Dongguan), Hong Kong, Vietnam and Malaysia. Meanwhile, the Powder Metallurgy Parts produced by JABON INTERNATIONAL are widely used in the field of automotive components except to the transmission market.

To combine the existing technology of IKKA and the advantage of its access to channels, JABON INTERNATIONAL accordingly expects to apply and to extend its own products to higher-margin fields, such as automobile application. Also, by using the pipeline of IKKA company's existing customers, our target is to integrate with different business fields, to deepen and broaden the portfolio.
